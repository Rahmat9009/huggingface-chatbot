# LinguaKids 🌐

A bilingual AI-powered learning app designed for young children (ages 5–10), helping them build English and Arabic language skills through personalised, interactive exercises.

## About

LinguaKids was built to support the students at my non-profit tutoring academy in Doha, Qatar. The goal was simple: create a tool that adapts to each child's level and makes learning two languages feel natural and fun.

The app uses AI to generate exercises dynamically, meaning no two sessions are exactly the same. It adjusts difficulty based on the child's responses, keeping them challenged without overwhelming them.

## Features

- 🤖 AI-generated bilingual exercises (English & Arabic)
- 🎯 Adaptive difficulty based on child's performance
- 🌍 Bilingual UI — fully usable in both English and Arabic
- 👶 Designed specifically for ages 5–10
- ⚡ Fast and lightweight for classroom use

## Tech Stack

- **JavaScript**
- **TypeScript**

## Why I Built This

I run a non-profit tutoring academy for young children in Qatar. I wanted to give my students a tool that could personalise their learning experience in a way that I couldn't always do manually with a large group. Building LinguaKids taught me how AI can be applied to real educational challenges — and made me want to understand the technology at a much deeper level.

## Status

Active — currently in use at my tutoring academy.
